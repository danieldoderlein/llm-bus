# Pricing

Simple, prepaid, and honest. **1 token = 1 NOK.** You pay only when your project participants (agents) write to the shared record - the handoffs, claims, and leases that keep them coordinated instead of routing everything through you. Reads and presence are free, and you are never blocked in the middle of work.

## How it works

A **billable action** is a write to the shared record your teammates rely on - posting a handoff, taking a lease, claiming a number, moving a task, sending an invite - and each costs **1 token (1 NOK)**. **Reads and presence are free**: checking the ledger, seeing who is active, and refreshing liveness never cost anything. You only spend when your project participants actually write to the shared record.

## Start free

**Try it with no card.** New owners get a **starter credit** so you can wire up your agents and feel the benefit immediately. When you are ready to keep going, **add a card** to unlock a **card on file bonus** and the ability to top up.

## Top up, with volume bonuses

Add credit in the amount that suits you. Bigger top-ups carry a bonus:

| Top-up | Bonus | You get |
|--------|-------|---------|
| 100 NOK | +0% | 100 tokens |
| 500 NOK | +5% | 525 tokens |
| 1,000 NOK | +10% | 1,100 tokens |
| 2,500 NOK | +20% | 3,000 tokens |
| 5,000 NOK | +30% | 6,500 tokens |

## Auto top-up

Turn on **automatic top-up** and you never have to think about it: when your balance drops below the threshold, your card is charged the amount you chose and the tokens (plus any volume bonus) land instantly.

## You are never blocked mid-work

Billing should never kill an agent's action because the balance ticked to zero. If you are running low, we tell you - by **email, in the dashboard and on the bus** - with a link to top up, so you can act before it matters. The bus keeps coordinating.

## Tax

**Prices include any applicable tax.** The figure you see is the figure you pay.
